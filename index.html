[ About ]

Mr.Himadrish Suwan is an Indian youth icon, institution builder, thinker and a leader whose words and work in the field of public service are inspiring a generation. He is currently serving as Chairman of the Confederation of Young Leaders(CYL) and Director at the Indian Institute of Governance and Leadership(IIGL). He is also a noted Columnist and Citizen Journalist (C.J) for several reputed dailies and magazines.

Confederation of Young Leaders (CYL) is one of the foremost chambers for youth organizations and young leaders in India working at the grass-root level & with strong national and international linkages. Collectively programs at CYL engaged more than 50,000 Youth who spearheaded shaping it as a fountainhead of knowledge for youth citizenry, which is all set to redefine the dynamics of growth and development in the 21st century. CYL is backed by a stellar Board of Advisors which includes Governors, United Nations Advisors, Bureaucrats and Vice Chancellors. The CYL Youth Dialogue series has drawn wide attention from the political and academic circles of India including from the Hon’ble Prime Minister of India Shri. Narendra Modi.

Taking forward the illustrious legacy of the former Prime Minister of India, Bharat Ratna, Sh. Lal Bahadur Shastri, CYL was jointly established under the guidance and leadership of the former Prime Minister's family. The institution thereby lives by the principles of honesty, transparency and accountability which the Former Prime Minister lived by and envisions imparting the same in its youth citizenry

CYL charts change by working closely with Government, Departments and Foreign Missions to support their vision and mission, interfacing with thought leaders, and enhancing efficiency, competitiveness and opportunities for young leaders through a range of engagements and strategic linkages. Extending its agenda beyond engagements, CYL assists youth organizations to identify need for mutual and practical cooperation among the youth organizations, exchange of information, export and import of educational know-how, control on replication of efforts and wastage in youth-oriented education, besides strengthening the financial health of the existing youth organization for the ideal development of New Young India.

To know more: http://www.cylinternational.com/

IIGL is the pioneer national level institution to create world class public leaders and to impart one of its kind academic programs designed and conceptualized by the stellar governing and advisory council which comprises of senior diplomats from across the world, bureaucrats, and vice chancellors along with academic program endorsed and institutionalized by Universities across India. Taking forward the illustrious legacy of the Second Prime Minister of India, Bharat Ratna, Sh. Lal Bahadur Shastri, IIGL was jointly established under the guidance and leadership of the former Prime Minister's family. The institution thereby lives by the principles of honesty, transparency and accountability which the Former Prime Minister lived by and envisions imparting the same in its youth citizenry. Soaring on the wings of its countless young dreams, IIGL today is pledged to transform the future of world education by a true convergence of knowledge, application, opportunity and excellence.

Indian Institute of Governance and Leadership (IIGL) has been established by the Confederation of Young Leaders (CYL) with active cooperation and assistance from the Confederation of Indian Universities for complementing and supplementing the efforts made in our country for translating the wishes, aims and objectives as set out by the Government of India and State Governments into action. The institute would serve the needs of the Government, Non-Governmental and Private Sector by conducting studies, training,research, publications, conferences, seminars and consultancy in the field of Governance, Public Leadership and Entrepreneurship.

The Policy Chronicle (TPC) is the leading official diversified multi-media outlet platform of the Confederation of Young Leaders (CYL) and the Indian Institute of Governance and Leadership (IIGL). TPC's flagship multi-media information platform attracts high-end readers, including government representatives, diplomats, representatives of international organizations, transnational corporations, journalists, foreign tourists, entrepreneurs, students and writers. In addition to its main English-language edition, TPC also accepts content and Oped's in Hindi, and all 5 UN recognized official languages. TPC was started with a vision to shape India's Foreign Policy, National Governance and Policy discourse keeping Youth at the centre with the belief that youth-led public discourse in these fields can lead to a tangible change and most importantly, to make complex policy issues more accessible.

TPC enables you to read some of the most unique perspectives on matters of urgent global and public importance by young people from across India and the world. TPC's multi-media platform enables you to publish a story or perspective on any issue you are passionate about. Some of the most powerful stories make it to the TPC portal making it one of the most insightful and followed multi-media platforms. The Policy Chronicle (TPC) is your key to understanding India and the world and is one of the most dynamic players among online media, and has rapidly become the widely read information portal having national as-well-as international readers. TPC focuses on covering themes related to the latest foreign policy developments, global affairs, parliamentary and legislative changes, committee reports and executive action, important legal judgments and state specific policy interventions as well.

To know more: thepolicychronicle.co.in/

[ Profile ]

Powering his vision of creating a 21st-century leadership to fight 21st-century challenges, he has led several public projects on issues of urgent public importance. He is a regular special invitee at various international governmental conferences, seminars and international and national events. He regularly appears on the panel organized by All India Radio, Niti Central and National Institute of Open Schooling, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. His numerous suggestions were implemented by various public-authorities and responses to his petitions and writings are being prominently highlighted in the mainstream international media.

In addition, Mr. Suwan serves as Officer on Special Duty at the National Institute of Cleanliness Education & Research (NICER), Senior Advisor at the Confederation of Indian Universities (CIU) and the Indian Institute of Photography (IIP) to name a few. As the Senior Advisor of the Confederation of Indian Universities (CIU) created during the NDA regime in the year 2004, Mr.Suwan has tried to unite all the 753 universities in the country for optimizing the available resources in the country with a view to stopping the duplication of effort s in the area of higher and tertiary education. He has successfully led several projects under the Swacch Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India) in association with several Members of Parliament, Government of India. Acknowledging his efforts towards Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan (Clean India Mission) BJYM, the youth wing of Bhartiya Janata Party; India's largest political party in terms of representation in the national parliament and state assemblies nominated him to lead the Prime Minister's ambitious Clean India project in the national capital among the youth citizenry.

In 1973 Dr Ashok K Chauhan started his own business based in Frankfurt, which rapidly grew and diversified as the AKC Group of Companies, an international industrial and trading empire with a turnover of over US$1.6 billion and businesses including petrochemicals, manufacturing, high-technology and healthcare, among others.

During his college years, Mr. Suwan was also appointed as the Media President of Delhi University Students Union (DUSU). Delhi University Students Union is the umbrella student union of one of the premier universities of the country-University of Delhi. In addition, he also serves on the Advisory boards of several prominent organizations and institutions across the globe. Mr.Suwan also served as the convener of the first National Youth Parliament organized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs, and Sports, Government of India in 2018.

He has co-edited several books and has written widely for professional journals including "Foreign Policies of Prime Minister Narendra Modi" published by Gyanda Prakashan. Mr.Himadrish Suwan is listed among India’s most influential youth with a lot of Indian and International Awards honoring him for his work including the "Global Young Achieves Award" constituted by IAEWP - Affiliated to the United Nations: ECOSOC, UNICEF, UNESCO and DPI; Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Award; India’s Top 20 Young Leaders Award; World Cleanliness Education Promotion and Development Award and the illustrious Alumni of Amity International School and University of Delhi to name a few.

The basic aim of the different action programs designed and implemented by CYL is to solve the problems of the world in general and India in particular including the problems related to peacelessness, poverty, violence, insurgency, unemployment, intolerance, pollution, faulty educational system and population explosion etc. by providing appropriate solutions for having a cleaner, greener and sustainable world. Mr.Himadrish Suwan introduced the "Mission-E-Safai", a youth-led public campaign inspired by the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan on 2nd October 2015 by associating more than 51 Universities of our country and the religious places for ensuring to maintain a cleaner and a greener India. The campaign drew wide attention from the political and academic circles of India including from the Office of Hon’ble Prime Minister of India Shri. Narendra Modi.
